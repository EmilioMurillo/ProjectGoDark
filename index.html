Project Go Dark

Mission

“Create the next generation of black entertainment industry professionals to project black art, black culture, and black lives”

Project GoDark Inc. in partnership with the Black Music Action Coalition consists of artists and their representatives who will work with our chosen benefactor, Historically Black Colleges and Universities, to develop programming relative to all aspects of the music business including the first industry wide summer internship and career development program specifically for HBCU students. Our board will design programs ranging from production and engineering to programs focused on marketing and branding through technology. Project Go Dark Inc. programs will be designed to cater to students of all ages to birth the next generation of music industry professionals.
